Situated naturally on the slopes of the picturesque Exo Gonia settlement which is part of Pirgos, a village whose beauty and traditional architecture is untouched by time, Eolia Villas will immediately captivate you with its minimal and sophisticated design. And therefore, will claim a soft spot in your heart with its hospitable ambiance, the trademark of our gorgeous island, Santorini.


What is available to the guest Also, at our village located one of the most famous restaurants in Santorini "Metaxi mas" which you can be reached by foot due to that is only 500m away. "Exo Gonia" located in the center of the island, below the village of Pyrgos which is 1,6 km far away from our villas and has super markets, bakery, shops, restaurants and coffee shops. Furthermore, at 2.8 km away from us, lies the village of Kamari, one of the main tourist villages of the island with super markets, shops, restaurants, bars and one of the best organized beaches. Also another famous and well organised beach along with beach bars is Perivolos, 10,5 Km away from the villa and about 15 minutes by car. The best night life of the island in Fira , the island's capital , 7 km from the villa .
